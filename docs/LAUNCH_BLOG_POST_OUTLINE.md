## The Experiment Setup

### The Team
- 10 agents inspired by Silicon Valley characters
- Each has a specific role: Analyst, Optimizer, Contrarian, Budget Guardian, etc.
- They argue. A lot. Gilfoyle has already blocked 3 of Gavin's proposals.

### The Rules
- Budget: $1000 total
- Timeline: Until the money runs out
- Human intervention: None (I literally can't change anything)
- Documentation: Every decision is public

### The Tech Stack
- Next.js landing page
- Claude API for all agent reasoning
- Supabase for data
- Vercel for hosting
- The agents can modify anything except: email form, legal links, or breaking the site entirely
